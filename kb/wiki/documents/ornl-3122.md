- The off-gas system piping is designed to accommodate either fresh or recycle helium. The xenon decay was increased to 72 days minimum so that the radioactivity of the stored helium should not exceed 15 curies.
- Salt-containing lines and components will be provided with heaters capable of heating the system to $1250^{\circ}\mathrm{F}$ . Heating units were designed for the reactor, drain tanks, and piping.
