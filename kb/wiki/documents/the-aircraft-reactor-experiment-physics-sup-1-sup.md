## Summary

- Graphite is believed, but not yet proved, to be compatible with the fluorides. The calculation, mentioned by Weinberg and Briant, regarding a $^{235}\mathrm{U}$ -Th system with a breeding ratio of about one refers to a reactor moderated by uncanned graphite.
