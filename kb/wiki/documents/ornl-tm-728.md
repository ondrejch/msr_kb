## Summary

- The final volumes of the report, Part XII, contain the rather extensive listings of working drawings, specifications, schedules, tabulations, etc. These have been given a more limited distribution.
- None of the information contained in this report is of a classified nature.
- The list of biographical references provides an indication of ORNL personnel contributing most valuably to the MSRE literature, and thus to this report.
- This report is one of a series that describes the design and operation of the Molten-Salt Reactor Experiment. All the reports are listed below.
- ORNL-TM-729 MSRE Design and Operations Report, Part II, Nuclear and Process Instrumentation, by J. R. Tallackson
- ORNL-TM-730* MSRE Design and Operations Report, Part III, Nuclear Analysis, by P. N. Haubenreich and J. R. Engel, B. E. Prince, and H. C. Claiborne
