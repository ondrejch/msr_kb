## Summary

- This report was prepared as an account of Government sponsored work. Neither the United States, nor the Commission, nor any person acting on behalf of the Commission:
- B. Assumes any liabilities with respect to the use of, or for damages resulting from the use of any information, apparatus, method, or process disclosed in this report.
- The design, procurement, and construction of the MSRE was $90\%$ complete on January 31, 1964.
- Except for the addition of a safety relay cabinet and the removal of one nuclear panel, the layout of the instrumentation and controls system remains the same as reported previously.
- Instrument applications diagrams, tabulations, and design drawings were revised to incorporate recent changes in, and additions to, the instrumentation and controls system.
- Designs of 46 of 53 panel board sections presently required are complete. The remaining 7 are partially complete.
