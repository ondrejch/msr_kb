## Summary

- Documents in the Nuclear Power Options Viability Study (NPOVS) bibliography are classified under one of four headings or categories as follows:
- The report of the NPOVS is organized into four volumes, as follows:
- The evaluative criteria established in this study are as follows:
- 1. The calculated risk to the public due to accidents is less than or equal to the calculated risk associated with the best modern LWRs.
- 2. The probability of events leading to loss of investment is less than or equal to $10^{-4}$ per year (based on plant costs).
- 3. The economic performance of the nuclear plant is at least equivalent to that for coal-fired plants. (Financial goals for the utility are met, and busbar costs are acceptable to the public utility commissions.)
