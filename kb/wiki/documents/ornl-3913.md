## Summary

- This report was prepared as an account of Government sponsored work. Neither the United States nor the Commission, nor any person acting on behalf of the Commission.
- B. Assume any liabilities with respect to the use of, or for damage resulting from the use of any information, apparatus, method, or process disclosed in this report.
- REACTOR CHEMISTRY DIVISION ANNUAL PROGRESS REPORT
- Results of a completed investigation of the high-temperature reactions of NaF and $\mathbf{ScF}_3$ show that the system is characterized by two eutectic and two peritectic reactions which occur in association with the intermediate phases 3NaF-ScF $_3$ and NaF-ScF $_3$ .
- An x-ray structure analysis has ascertained the formula of this compound, previously designated 7LiF-6UF<sub>4</sub>. In it are found UF<sub>8</sub> polyhedra which are quite similar to those in crystalline UF<sub>4</sub>.
- The previously determined structure of $(\mathrm{NH}_4)_2\mathrm{MnF}_5$ was refined by least squares.
