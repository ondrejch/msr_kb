- Two molten-salt convection loops have been operated in beam hole HN-1 of the Oak Ridge Research Reactor. Both loops contained molten-fluoride fuel salt ( $^{7}\mathrm{LiF - BeF}_2 - \mathrm{ZrF}_4 - \mathrm{UF}_4$ ) with enriched uranium varying in concentration up to 2.1 mole%.
- Successful operation of the major heating, cooling, temperature control, and sampling systems was demonstrated; however, both loop experiments were terminated because of breaks in the primary loop systems.
