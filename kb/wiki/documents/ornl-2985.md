## Summary

- This report was prepared as an account of Government sponsored work. Neither the United States, nor the Commission, nor any person acting on behalf of the Commission!
- B. Assumes any liabilities with respect to the use of, or for damages resulting from the use of any information, apparatus, method, or process disclosed in this report.
- A study has been made of the feasibility of constructing a small nuclear power plant capable of operating one year completely unattended. A system which will produce 1 Mw of electricity without interruption is required for field use in about four years.
- The authors conclude that it is feasible to develop a reliable, simplified, pressurized-water reactor system for unattended service in the allotted time.
- For the present study the following system requirements were established:
- 1. The plant is to operate unattended at full power continuously for a minimum of one year.
